About Pub

Irish Pub in Borisov is waiting for great lovers of good food and beer, all those who know how to have a good time.

Pub - is a place where people come to relax and have a glass of beer after work, to spend time with their friends. The most common phrase in Pub is "For me as usual". Here you can dine in the evening, taste exquisite drinks and meet old or new friends.

Our Pub offers the wide range of draft and bottled beers. Dark and light, ale and lager, strong and non-alcoholic - everyone can find something to enjoy in the Pub menu. Various beer snacks, meat and fish steaks, burgers will complement your dinner.

In addition, the Pub has a great entertainment option for any company. Table for pool billiards, two dartboards, table football (kicker) are available for all guests of our restaurant. In Irish Pub you can watch broadcasts of different sporting events, celebrate your personal occasion or join the celebration of such international festivals and holidays as Halloween, St. Patrick's Day, Arthur Guinness Day and others. Every year on June 15 the big celebration of Irish Pub’s birthday takes place.

In the Pub we create the traditions together. So let your visit to ours become a good tradition!

Irish Pub in Borisov is a great rest for the best friends!
